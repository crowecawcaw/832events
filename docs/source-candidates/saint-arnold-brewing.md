Major Houston brewery and beer garden. Events are published as ad-hoc
WordPress posts/pages — no calendar plugin, no ICS feed, no Eventbrite
organizer page. Third-party events at the venue appear under individual
organizer pages (not Saint Arnold's own). Low event volume at the brewery
level.

Not viable as a structured calendar source without an HTML scraper against
fragile static posts. The 365thingsinhouston.com Localist aggregator includes
Saint Arnold events but is itself an aggregator, not a canonical source.

Re-evaluate if they add a calendar platform or Eventbrite organizer account.
