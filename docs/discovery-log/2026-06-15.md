### Key findings

- **Two parks with confirmed ICS feeds**: Hermann Park and Levy Park both explicitly support calendar subscriptions including iCalendar (.ics) format. Both are active with 30+ events across various categories (fitness, cultural, family, free programming). High confidence tier — ready for technical verification of ICS feed URLs.

- **Sports teams available via CalendarLabs**: Houston Astros and Rockets both have confirmed ICS feeds available through CalendarLabs (calendarlabs.com), which acts as a gateway to sports team schedules. These feeds work with standard calendar applications (Outlook, Google Calendar, iOS, Android). Texans also likely available but needs verification.

- **Neighborhood/district calendars**: Ion District (tech hub, downtown) and Midtown Houston Management District both have active event calendars with moderate volume (~10-20 events/month). These need investigation to identify the underlying calendar platform and ICS feed URLs.
