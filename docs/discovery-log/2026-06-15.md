### Investigation summary

**InPrint Houston** — Literary arts nonprofit with book clubs, author reading series, poetry events. Calendar powered by The Events Calendar WordPress plugin. Feed verification successful at `https://inprinthouston.org/calendar/?ical=1` — returns valid VCALENDAR format with events through 2026. Low maintenance platform, stable feed expected.

**Bad Astronaut Brewery** — 36,000 sq ft brewery/taproom north of downtown with 35+ events. Recurring programming: happy hours (Wed-Sun), run club (Wed), air hockey (Thu). The Events Calendar plugin with ICS export/Google Calendar subscription options confirmed. Geo: 29.7683, -95.3628.

**The Mood Room** — Houston comedy venue (EaDo/East End neighborhood) with weekly Thursday open mics and special performances. Squarespace events feature with ICS/Google Calendar export confirmed on each event listing. 21+ bar, free open mic admission.
